AI's Global Impact: Industry-Wide Insights Through Data

This project explores the multifaceted influence of artificial intelligence across global industries using a comprehensive dataset of 200 observations spanning countries, industries, and key metrics from 2020 to 2025.

Through exploratory data analysis and visualization with Python (Seaborn, Matplotlib, Statsmodels), the project uncovers how AI adoption affects job displacement, revenue growth, content generation, and human-AI collaboration. Key relationshipssuch as the correlation between AI adoption and job loss, or shifts in consumer trust over timeare visualized using dual-axis plots, violin plots, and time trends.

This analysis helps stakeholders, from policymakers to tech leaders, better understand where AI is accelerating valueand where it raises concern.

Key Focus Areas:

AI adoption rates by country and industry

Job loss vs. revenue gains from AI

Consumer trust trends

Regulation status and tool usage

Human-AI collaboration metrics

Tech stack: Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Statsmodels Data: AI Content Impact Dataset (200 entries, 12 features)
